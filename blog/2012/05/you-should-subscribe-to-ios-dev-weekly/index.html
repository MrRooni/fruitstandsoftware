You Should Subscribe to iOS Dev Weekly

My good friend Dave Verwer has taken it upon himself to keep the iOS development community informed of the latest and greatest happenings, one weekly newsletter at a time.

Each newsletter is concise, easily digestible, and always contains at least one link that I want to read. If you’re not already subscribed you should head over to iosdevweekly.com, put your email address in the box, and click Subscribe.
